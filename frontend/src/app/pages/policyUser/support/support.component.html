Frequently Asked Questions

How can I pay my premium online?

You can pay your premium by visiting the Payments section and entering your policy details.

How do I check my policy status?

Go to the Policies section to view your policy details, including status and due dates.

What should I do if I miss my premium due date?

You can revive your policy by contacting our support or visiting the nearest LIC branch.
